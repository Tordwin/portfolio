Welcome to Edwin's Top Songs

Lagoon - Rich Brian

Lagoon sang and produced by Rich Brian
Released in 2022 with the album of Brightside, Lagoon brings a rich and fastbeat style of rap that Rich Brian has adapted and evolved. Alongside other songs like Getcho Mans, Sunny and New Tooth, Lagoon punches you in the face as when you press play and gives you the chills as it slows down towards the end.

Runnin - 21 Savage

Runnin sang by 21 Savage. Produced By Metro Boomin
Released in 2020 with the album Savage Mode II, Runnin brings a hard hitting rap song to drive with. The combination of the bass and the lyrics makes you feel as if you are racing through the streets. This song never fails to make me feel good. Give it a listen when you wanna break the speed limits.

eventide - kudasaibeats

eventide produced by kudasaibeats
Released in 2018 eventide is another hit with kudasaibeats. Recently released with afterthoughts (unreleased singles) his Lo-Fi exploration allows the calmness to hit the listener through the bass and the rythmetic guitar. Even as a loop, it gives me the calmness as I am driving in the middle of the night.
